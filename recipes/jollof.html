Jollof Rice

Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 medium tomatoes, roughly chopped
  • 1/2 medium Scotch bonnet pepper, stem removed
  • 1/2 medium onion, roughly chopped
  • 3 small red bell peppers, roughly chopped
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 11/2 teaspoons hot ground chilli pepper, such as African dried chilli or cayenne
  • 11/2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon plus 1 heaping teaspoon onion powder
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 tablespoon dried thyme
  • 21/2 cups medium-grain rice
